Free Fire me rank push karne ke tips

Posted on Feb 28, 2025

Rank push karte waqt strategy, character choice aur teamwork sab matter karta hai. Neeche kuch practical tips:

1. Right character pick karein

Alok, K, Skyler jaise characters survival aur rotation me help karte hain. Squad me ek healer/utility zaroor rakhein.

2. Drop location

Shuru me safe zone ke andar drop karein taaki early fight na ho. Mid-game me rotation pe focus karein.

3. Communication

Mic on rakhein, callouts dein — enemy position, loot, zone movement. Coordination se win rate badhti hai.
